Sports Creative Portfolio

Covering sports with a focus on real moments, strong visuals, and clear storytelling.

I’m Oliver Zureick, a sports media student at Miami University with a focus on photography, creative content, and visual storytelling. My work is built around live competition, athlete-focused imagery, and design that feels intentional instead of generic.

I want the work I make to feel polished without losing what made the moment worth capturing in the first place. Whether I’m shooting baseball, basketball, swimming, or building promotional graphics, the goal stays the same: make content that feels sharp, honest, and worth remembering.
